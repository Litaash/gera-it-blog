Our team at Gera–IT has some exciting news to announce! Our CEO just got off the phone with our analyst from Clutch, who let us know that Clutch just awarded us as one of the top developers for 2020! Investing in your business is more important now than ever, which is why we’re honored to be listed as one of Ukraine’s best mobile app and web developers for 2020 by Clutch!

“It’s a great honor for us to be chosen as one of the leaders among b2b development companies in Ukraine. Thank you!” – Vadym Karpenko, CEO at Gera-IT

Clutch is the world’s leading B2B ratings and reviews platform! Located in the heart of the historic DuPont Circle neighborhood of Washington DC, Clutch has a dedicated team who independently verifies each and every review that they conduct with verified clients from around the world. Clutch is the gold standard for ratings and reviews for B2B service providers, which is why we’re incredibly honored that Clutch’s list of top 2020 companies shows us as one of the best mobile app and web developers in all of Ukraine!
